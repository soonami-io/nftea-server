Story of the nftea

Once upon a time, in a far-off land, there were six different types of tea - Black tea, White tea, Green tea, Rooibos tea, Pu-eah tea and Oolong tea. They lived in their separate tea gardens and were content with their daily routines. But one day, they heard about the wonderful world of web3 and decided to go on a journey together to explore it.

Green tea was excited about the trip because he had heard that web3 was a place where everything was transparent and fair. Black tea was curious about the new technology and wanted to learn more about it. Oolong tea was eager to see how it could benefit emerging markets, and Rooibos tea was just happy to be going on an adventure with her friends.

The four teas set off on their journey, crossing mountains and valleys, until they finally reached the entrance to the web3 world - a soonami.io discord server.

Get to know your makers

soonami.io is a launchpad for the best projects and teams in web3 with a strong belief in a decentralized future, zero-knowledge, and the power of DAOs, building teams and funding projects working towards a decentralized world, and it is the best place to be, build and grow! Within its discord server, soonami connects with its community to help founders scale their businesses and educate those who are curious about blockchain.

One of the ventures accelerated by soonami is Unbounded Dao, a platform providing an agnostic toolchain for the web3 ecosystem. One of its products is mQuark, which offers interoperability and a blueprint-style schema for NFTs. mQuark allows users to mint any object as an NFT, upgrade it, and attach properties to it for use across any web3 or metaverse platform, turning it into a functional asset that can be staked, used as collateral, or involved in DeFi activities.

soonami used mQuark as technology to provide you NFTea - an invitation to come and drink a cup of tea together! Mint NFTea and join the web3 revolution at the soonami discord server.
