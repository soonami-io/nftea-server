Story of the nftea

Once upon a time, in a land where teas were kings, six unique ones lived together in harmony. Black tea, White tea, Green tea, Rooibos tea, Pu-erh tea, and Oolong tea each had their own special qualities and lived in separate tea gardens, content with their daily routines. But one day, they heard whispers of a revolutionary new world, a place where they could become more than just tea - the world of Web3.

Excited by the prospect of becoming something truly unique, the teas decided to embark on a journey together to discover this magical land. Along the way, they gathered special ingredients to enhance their flavors and make themselves stand out from the rest.

Finally, they arrived at the entrance to the Web3 world - a sprawling marketplace filled with opportunities and challenges. As they explored this new and exciting place, they discovered that they could use blockchain technology to create unique digital representations of themselves, each with their own special blend of ingredients.

The teas worked tirelessly, perfecting their digital creations and blending themselves with the finest ingredients. And soon, they were transformed into living, breathing pieces of art, each with its own unique flavor and personality.

As they explored the Web3 world, they encountered other digital creations and formed friendships with them. They learned that they could trade their digital representations with others and that their creations would live forever, becoming a part of Web3's rich history.

And so, the six teas lived happily ever after, continuing to explore the wonders of web3 and sharing their unique creations with the world. They knew that they would never forget their journey and the lessons they had learned along the way.

Get to know your makers

Flying Fish Tea Co. came to soonami with an intriguing idea of utilizing their quality teas and unique style to raise funds for those in need through the power of blockchains and NFTs. We were only too happy to oblige and just as we completed the project we couldn’t think of a more worthwhile cause than the current tragedy after an earthquake in Turkey.

Soonami is a launchpad that provides infrastructure for Web3 projects with a strong belief in a decentralized future, zero-knowledge, and the power of DAOs. Building tools and architecture of the future is what we do.

NFTea is the first project that utilizes our end-to-end suite of tools that make a journey through Web3 product build easier, transparent, and efficient:

  • Conceived and tokenized using Foundance - a tool that helps to turn an idea into a business through a smart contract-based agreement.
  • NFTs were created using mQuark which is part of Unbounded.vision initiative, a tool that offers interoperability and blueprint-style schema for NFTs. mQuark allows users to mint any object as an NFT, upgrade it, and attach properties to it for use across any Web3 or metaverse platform, turning it into a functional asset with true utility, not just a collectible GIF image.
  • Finally, the project was audited using our AuditOne - all-in-one auditing platform, ensuring security and diligence.
